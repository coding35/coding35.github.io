There are three general types of data used in machine learning.(Numerical, Categorical, Ordinal)

  • Numerical
  • Categorical
  • Ordinal

Numerical

  • Represents some sort of quantitative measurement
    • Height
    • Weight
    • Temperature
  • Numerical data consists of two types.
    • Discrete
      • Discrete or integer based. The count of some occurrence. A finite count. ex (1,4,5, 12,600)
        • Number of children
        • Number of cars
        • Flip of coin
    • Continuous
      • Continuous Data. An infinite range of possible values. ex (1.20, 1.21, 1.23)
        • Temperature
        • Time for some event
        • Wind speed

Categorical

  • Represents some sort of qualitative measurement that has no inherent mathematic meaning. These numbers are a shorthand to categorically represent an object or construct.
    • Color
    • Yes/no
    • Product Category
    • Political Party

Ordinal

  • A mixture of numerical and categorical data where the categorical data has a mathematical meaning. A good example of this would be movie ratings. In a ranking a of 1-5, 5 is a better rating than one. These rankings have mathematical meaning.
    • Rating
    • Ranking
    • Grade
