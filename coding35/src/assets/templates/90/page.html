The DMUX or demultiplexer is a digital logic gate that takes a single input and forwards it to one of the many outputs.

HDL

        CHIP DMux {
            IN in, sel;
            OUT a, b;
        
            PARTS:
            Not(in=sel, out=c);
            And(a=c, b=in, out=a);
            And(a=sel, b=in, out=b);
        }
